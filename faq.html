General questions

What is Yuvraj motors?

Yuvraj motors acts as a bridge between car owners and automobile service stations, our mechanics thoroughly assess the service aspects and help you in cutting down any extra service costs that you might incur due to lack of knowledge.

What services does "Yuvraj motors" offer?

As we operate in after-hours, our services include the following only:
  • Scheduled service
  • Car care
  • Roadside assistance

How to book a service request?

You can book a service request through the following means:
  1. From our website or app homepage
  2. After logging in to your account on our website or app
  3. By calling us on our Toll Free no.
  4. Email us at : info@yuvrajmotors.in

How do I make changes to an already booked request?

You can make changes to an already booked service request by logging into your account.

Will you get the car picked or do I need to drop the car?

You could either get your car picked by our verified valet, the car pickup is payable Rs XX. In case you drop your car to our service station, Yuvraj motors will gladly take you back to your office or home, if it is within a 5 Km radius of the branch. When booking in your car, please make the branch aware that you will require a lift back to your office or home in the morning as to allow them to make the necessary arrangements. Please make arrangements to collect your vehicle in the afternoon as we are unable to fetch customers and bring them back to the branch.
